Expresolv Limited (Formerly known as Expresolv Pvt. Ltd) is incorporate in June 2016 with the aim to sale bulk fine chemicals, speciality chemicals and excipient grade chemicals to customers at competitive price with technical support and regulatory documents.

Expresolv an ISO 9001 certified, dynamically growing company, investing in technological development and setting up of a modern infrastructure including construction of state-of-the-art manufacturing unit and quality unit.

The company has strong foothold in the local Pharmaceuticals companies.

The company has been developing and manufacturing speciality chemicals and pharmaceuticals excipients at its own unit situated at Narol, Ahmedabad, Gujarat in india and selling and distributing these in over 10 to 15 states in India and 2-3 countries.
